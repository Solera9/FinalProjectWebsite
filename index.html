About Macabre Manor


Tradition since 1960s

Welcome to Le Macabre Manor, where the eerie and elegant intertwine to create an unforgettable dining experience. Nestled in the heart of the city, our restaurant transports you to a gothic mansion shrouded in mystery and dark charm. From the moment you step through our creaking doors, you are enveloped in an atmosphere that evokes the spine-tingling allure of classic horror.

At Le Macabre Manor, our décor is a hauntingly beautiful blend of Victorian opulence and macabre intrigue. Flickering candlelight casts ghostly shadows on walls adorned with antique portraits and sinister relics, while our staff, clad in period attire, adds to the immersive experience with their enigmatic presence. Whether you are a horror aficionado or simply seeking an extraordinary night out, our restaurant promises an experience that lingers long after the final bite. Dare to dine in the dark elegance of Le Macabre Manor, where every meal is a macabre masterpiece.
